Motorway rest areas in Morocco are essential stops for travelers en route. These strategically located spaces along highways offer various services, such as gas stations, restaurants, impeccable toilets and sometimes recreational areas. They allow drivers and passengers to take a well-deserved break, eat and get back on the road in complete safety.

Jawaz Autoroute is an electronic toll system used on highways in Morocco. It allows drivers to pay toll fees electronically, without having to stop at traditional toll gates. Thanks to Jawaz Autoroute, users can cross reserved lanes quickly, which helps to reduce congestion and streamline traffic on Moroccan highways. This system is based on electronic devices installed in vehicles, which allow automatic detection of toll charges, thus providing a more convenient and efficient driving experience for travelers in Morocco.

The assistance service on Morocco's highways is a key element of road safety in the country. Available along major highways, this service provides rapid assistance in the event of a breakdown, accident or any other problem encountered by drivers. Support teams are typically equipped to provide breakdown services, light repairs, and medical assistance when needed. They intervene quickly to guarantee the safety of drivers and passengers, thus helping to maintain traffic flow and ensure safe travel on Moroccan highways.
